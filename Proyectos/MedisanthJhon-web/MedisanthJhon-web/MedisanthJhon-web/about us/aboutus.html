About Us

About us

We're a company specialized in databases of medical information for the public in order to create awareness about the area of medicine, through an interactive application.

Mission

Our mission is to be the number one healthcare application for our services, solutions and information that we provide to the user.

Our application offer information on news, health, suggestions and some advice or recommendations.

To be recognized for our wide range of quality information that we provide to our users.

Vission

Become the main health information center for our services.

How started MedisanthJhon?

We started in a virtual classroom looking for ways to help our community with the resources we have.

This is how MedisathJhon arises, seeking to help and keep people informed in the field of health.
